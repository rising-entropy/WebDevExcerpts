The Missile Man of India

Abdul Kalams' first Presidential Speech.

Here's a time-line of Dr. Kalams' Life.

  • 1931 Born in Rameshwaram, India.
  • 1954 Graduates in Physics from Saint Josephs'.
  • 1960 Gets a degree in Aeronautical Engineering from Madras Institute of Technology.
  • 1969 Transferred to ISRO from DRDO.
  • 1980 India enters Space-Club under Dr. Kalams' stewardship.
  • 1980-1999 Worked in development of IG Missiles and became an advisor to the PM of India.
  • 1999-2001 Principal Scientific Advisor to the Govt. of India.
  • 2002-2007 Becomes the President of India.
  • 2015 dr. abdul Kalam passes away.
